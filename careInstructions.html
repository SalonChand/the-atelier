Caring for
Your Pieces

A garment treated with care becomes better with time. These guidelines will help your Atelier pieces develop their full character across years of wear.

By Fabric

Care by Material

Each fabric in our collection has its own temperament. Follow these guidelines specific to your garment's material for the best results. Always check the care label inside your piece first.

Wool & Cashmere

✓ DoDry clean or hand wash in cold water with wool-specific detergent
✓ DoLay flat to dry on a clean towel away from heat
✓ DoSteam to remove creases — never iron directly
✗ Don'tTumble dry or wring — this distorts the fibres permanently
✗ Don'tHang on a hanger when wet — store folded only

Silk & Crepe

✓ DoDry clean only, or hand wash with silk-specific wash in cool water
✓ DoPress on reverse with a cool iron and a pressing cloth
✓ DoStore in a cotton garment bag away from direct light
✗ Don'tApply perfume or hairspray directly onto silk
✗ Don'tRub stains — blot gently with a clean, damp cloth

Linen & Cotton

✓ DoMachine wash on a gentle, cool cycle (30°C maximum)
✓ DoIron whilst slightly damp for best results
✓ DoDry naturally — linen softens beautifully with each wash
✗ Don'tWash above 30°C — this causes shrinkage and colour loss
✗ Don'tBleach or use optical brighteners

Structured Wool Blends

✓ DoDry clean only for all structured tailored pieces
✓ DoBrush with a soft clothes brush after each wear
✓ DoStore on a wide, shaped hanger to preserve shoulder structure
✗ Don'tOver-clean — dry cleaning too frequently weakens fibres
✗ Don'tCompress in luggage without a garment bag

Jersey & Matte Fabrics

✓ DoHand wash in cool water or machine wash inside-out on gentle cycle
✓ DoReshape whilst damp and dry flat
✓ DoStore folded rather than hung to avoid stretching
✗ Don'tTumble dry on high heat — causes permanent distortion
✗ Don'tIron directly — use a pressing cloth or steam only

Water-Resistant Gabardine

✓ DoSpot clean minor marks with a damp cloth and mild soap
✓ DoHang immediately after rain to dry naturally
✓ DoRe-proof with a fabric DWR spray once yearly
✗ Don'tMachine wash — this strips the water-resistant coating
✗ Don'tIron the outer shell — heat damages the coating

Longevity

Storage & Preservation

◈

Breathable Garment Bags

Store all pieces in cotton or linen garment bags — never plastic. Plastic traps moisture and accelerates fibre breakdown. Our pieces are delivered with a cotton dust bag for this purpose.

◫

Correct Hanger Shape

Structured coats and jackets should be hung on wide, shaped wooden hangers that support the shoulder seams. Knitwear and draped pieces should always be folded flat.

◧

Moth Prevention

Use natural cedar blocks or lavender sachets near wool and cashmere. Replace every 3–4 months. Avoid direct contact with garments — wrap cedar in cloth if placing inside a bag.

◨

Rest Between Wears

Allow garments to rest for at least 24 hours between wears. This gives fibres time to recover their shape and allows any moisture from body heat to dissipate fully.
