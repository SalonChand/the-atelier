Find Your
Perfect Fit

Our pieces are constructed with precision. Taking a few accurate measurements ensures your garment moves with you exactly as intended.

Step One

How to Measure

Use a soft measuring tape held close to the body — not tight, not loose. Measure over underwear only. Have someone assist you for the most accurate results. All measurements below are in centimetres.

1

Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor. Keep your arms relaxed at your sides.

2

Waist

Measure around your natural waist — the narrowest part of your torso, typically 2–3 cm above your navel.

3

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat, approximately 20 cm below your natural waist.

4

Shoulder Width

Measure from the tip of one shoulder across to the tip of the other, following the curve of your back.

5

Sleeve Length

From the shoulder tip to your wrist bone, with your arm slightly bent. This ensures correct cuff placement.

6

Inseam

From the crotch seam down to the floor along the inner leg. Stand straight with feet slightly apart.

Fit Philosophy

Our Approach to Fit

◈

Between Sizes

If your measurements fall between two sizes, we recommend sizing up for coats and structured pieces, and sizing down for draped or bias-cut garments.

◫

Fabric Behaviour

Our silk and crepe pieces have natural drape and ease. Wool and structured fabrics are cut with precise allowances — refer to the shoulder measurement above all else.

◧

Still Unsure?

Our client advisors are available to assist with personal fit consultations. Contact us and we will guide you to the correct size based on your exact measurements.
